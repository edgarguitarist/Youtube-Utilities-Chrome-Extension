Youtube Utilities

Available Actions 🔮✨

  • Press "A" to Like a video 👍🏽
  • Press "D" to Dislike a video 👎🏽
  • Press "S" to Subscribe to a Channel 😍
  • Press "X" to Add Like & Dislike buttons to Video Player 📺
  • Press "N" to Create a interval timer to skip ads ⛔
